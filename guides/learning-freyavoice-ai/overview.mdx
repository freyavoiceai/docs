## Introduction to Freyavoice AI

Freyavoice AI is a comprehensive voice AI platform designed to help you build, deploy, and manage intelligent voice agents and workflows. This learning path will guide you through understanding the platform's architecture, core concepts, and best practices for building production-ready voice AI solutions.

## What You'll Learn

This learning section covers everything you need to become proficient with Freyavoice AI:

- **Platform Architecture**: Understanding how Freyavoice AI works under the hood
- **Core Concepts**: Deep dive into agents, workflows, and telephony systems
- **Best Practices**: Proven patterns and strategies for building effective voice AI solutions
- **Advanced Techniques**: Optimization, scaling, and troubleshooting
